Inedible:  Liquidated nuances meant for another. Swaths of weathering glitches roost in graveled circumferences. Wherefore. Therefore. Thereupon. Caught by caricatures, parodies, unease swerving toward pawned omissions. Quickness hurries. Treading fermented shutterings lapse as interfusable thickets permeate. He said incompleteness was misunderstood. Broadly absent but readily concurring, I lay a fallen starfish on a desolate fringe. Urges preside over whims.  Preferences long for requests. Cylindrical scars induce coarseness that grinds before striking. Squint and you’ll probably miss it.
